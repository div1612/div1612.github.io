About

I am a full-time Ph.D. scholar in the Department of Computer Science at IIIT Delhi. I work under the supervision of Dr. Arun Balaji Buduru. I am particularly interested in working on research problems involving Speech processing, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). My current research focuses on developing cost-effective solutions to mitigate linguistic bias in some of the existing speaker verification systems using DL and NLP concepts. I successfully cleared my Ph.D. comprehensive exam on the 19th of August, 2022 . Before joining the Ph.D. program in August 2019, I worked as a Teaching Fellow in the Department of Computer Science at IIITD between August 2018 to July 2019.

Research Interests:
Speech Processing, Natural Language Processing, Human-Computer Interaction
